  • 区块信息
    • 获取区块信息
      调用方法： BlockList/页码/每页显示条数/日期 页码为整形，条数为整形，日期年为4位整形，月为2位整形，日为2位整形
      如：../BlockList/1/20/2022-01-16 意为获取2022年 1月 16日的，第1页的信息，每页显示20条
      返回json数据

      {"blockTotal":842,"pageNumber":421,"blockList":[{"id":1114740,"height":28677,"reward_address":"20m02ft8tk86g2hc4r45ac1xp5emp1qwdyv60k2vx456mx434q6nkb78g","reward_money":0,"time":"2022-01-16 14:01:29","hash":"00007005bd27a1419c45e3cee907fd17de5310fd771fb5a5fb5de6978d1280d1","type":"subsidiary-dpos"},{"id":1114739,"height":28676,"reward_address":"20m02ft8tk86g2hc4r45ac1xp5emp1qwdyv60k2vx456mx434q6nkb78g","reward_money":0,"time":"2022-01-16 14:00:29","hash":"0000700448381656a1e56714df7cf5821f09f2dcbeab71081383976dae792102","type":"subsidiary-dpos"}]}

      blockTotal: 符合条件的总记录数
      pageNumber：符合条件的总页数
      blockList:区块信息列表
      height:高度
      reward_address：广播方
      reward_money：挖矿收益
      time：出块时间
      hash：块哈希
      type: 出块类型
